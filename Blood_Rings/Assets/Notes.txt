Characters have Main Stats the can get upgraded
	Characters have fighting styles that can be swapped between mid fight
		Styles are variations on the Characters Main stats
		Eg. Main stats.
			WalkSpeed = 10;
			JumpHeight = 5;
			JumpFloat = 4;
			
			Style 1.
			WalkSpeed = 3;
			JumpHeight = 2;
			JumpFloat = 4;
